C48_StandUp
# Description: robot stands up after falling.

Known bugs:
* for now, the robot doesnt stand up due to stability problems,
the movements are the first it should do to stand.

Conditions:
* works with version 1.3 of drcsim!
* the function, for now, is for the robot lying on its stomach,
this will be changed to fit every falling position. 

*Not final steps, may change*
Follow the steps:
1. roslaunch atlas_utils atlas.launch
2. run designer and executer.
3. rosrun C48_StandUp C48_Node.py
4. in designer load tree located in TestTree folder.
5. run the tree.

Contains:
TestTree folder: tree wich executes the standing code.
